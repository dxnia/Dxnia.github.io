Research

I spent the larger part of my freshman year researching Social Anxiety and Generalized Anxiety Disorder through eye-tracking Dot-Probe therapy sessions. From this research, I, along with my partner, designed & structured a gamified app that would mimic the Dot-Probe therapy for social anxiety patients. We then taught ourselves C# & Unity3D in order to translate our design into code. It is now under trial testing at the UM health clinic.
